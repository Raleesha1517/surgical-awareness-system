Walk about 30 minutes per day at least 5 days per week. Walk at a comfortable speed but one that makes you sweat due to exertion. If you have access to a staircase, climb up and down two flights of steps twice a day. Gradually increase your level of exercise - do not over exert yourself suddenly A Caution: If you have heart orlung disease, get a chest pain or become excessively breathless stop and consult your doctor about exercise plan
